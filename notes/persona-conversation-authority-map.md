# Persona Conversation Authority Map

## Objective

Let a human address an Epiphany Persona in Discord, have that pressure enter the
resident swarm's own heartbeat physiology, run through the native
Projector -> Persona -> Interpreter loop, and return a governed reply to the
same conversation without VoidBot owning cognition, scheduling, or Persona
state.

## Owner

- Bifrost owns Discord transport identity, inbound target admission, actor and
  guild/channel binding, signatures, and outbound delivery receipts.
- Heartbeat owns durable conversation obligations, CTB opportunity, active-turn
  exclusion, and completion-gated cooldown.
- Imagination owns the Persona Projector. Persona owns natural speech. Mind's
  Interpreter owns conversion into typed candidate effects.
- Mind owns durable Persona-state admission. Epiphany owns the signed speech
  request and its eligibility. Bifrost owns the token-bearing transport worker
  and alone proves external delivery.

VoidBot is reference lineage and may remain the room moderator. It owns no
writer or scheduler in this path.

## Inputs

- A Bifrost-observed Discord event addressed to the configured Epiphany Persona.
- Exact guild, channel, message, reply target, actor, target runtime/repository,
  target Persona, content digest, and visible prompt.
- Current Persona state and semantic recall, recent home-repo activity, raw
  conversation context, social affordances, and organ dependencies.
- Root-owned model configuration, two dedicated service-identity anchors, and
  the canonical swarm brake.

## Outputs

- One signed Bifrost conversation delivery.
- One immutable Epiphany ingress receipt and one durable pending mention.
- One typed Persona turn request with exact reserved mention cargo.
- Exact Projector, Persona, and Interpreter stage receipts.
- A closed typed Interpreter-effects document.
- Optional admitted Persona self-state mutation.
- Optional signed Epiphany speech request and signed Bifrost Discord receipt.
- One terminal Persona-turn receipt that consumes, retains, or quarantines
  every mention and starts heartbeat cooldown.

## Derived State

- Discord/operator summaries, Aquarium bubbles, schedule JSON, model stream
  events, semantic recall, and speech previews are sight or cargo. None owns
  admission, delivery, or mention consumption.
- Qdrant is retrieval evidence, not Persona memory authority.

## Forbidden Writers

- VoidBot may not write Epiphany conversation obligations or select Persona
  turns.
- Resident Self feedback pressure may not impersonate addressed conversation.
- The operator-command path may not become ordinary chat ingress.
- Heartbeat may not delete a mention merely because it selected a turn.
- Model output may not mutate Mind or claim delivery directly.
- The Discord mouth may not synthesize Persona speech.
- Epiphany may not read Discord credentials or invoke a token-bearing bridge.
- An indeterminate or terminally failed external delivery may not return to the
  schedulable mention queue under a fresh request id.
- Schedule/artifact files may not become the only surviving turn request.

## Shared Paths

Direct mentions, role mentions, replies to a Persona message, retries after
restart, heartbeat selection, model failures, explicit silence/drop, state
mutation, public reply, and delivery reconciliation use the same typed ingress,
turn reservation, Interpreter admission, and terminal commit primitives.

## Cut Line

1. Replace the Bifrost/VoidBot observation dependency with Bifrost-native
   Discord ingress and delete literal VoidBot observer authority.
2. Admit each signed delivery once into a durable mention obligation.
3. Atomically reserve mention cargo when heartbeat selects `persona_turn`; do
   not consume it before terminal disposition.
4. Execute Projector, Persona, and Interpreter as typed model stages. Interpreter
   emits a strict schema, not text blocks parsed by rules.
5. Publish one immutable signed Epiphany request into its single-writer crossing
   store. Let the Bifrost-owned worker publish one signed receipt into the
   opposite single-writer store; no Discord credential crosses that boundary.
6. Quarantine exact conversational pressure after a signed `failed` or
   `unknown` receipt so automatic scheduling cannot retry an external
   consequence whose outcome is terminal or indeterminate.
7. Commit terminal outcome, delivery evidence, mention disposition, and
   heartbeat completion together or through replay-safe exact predecessors.

## Verification Layer

The full-path proof observes: duplicate Discord event -> one obligation -> CTB
selection -> no private substrate leakage -> natural Persona turn -> typed
effects -> exact Mind admission -> Bifrost reply to the source message ->
delivery receipt -> terminal mention disposition. Negative proofs cover brake,
crash before/after each stage, invalid model output, replay, wrong target,
foreign guild/channel, bot/self input, unknown delivery outcome, and service
restart.
