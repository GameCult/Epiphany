# Organ Dependency Contracts

Objective: every sub-agent depends on all the other sub-agents. No organ is a
little sovereign state. Each one owns a boundary, and each one must feel the
pressure of the others before acting.

## Standing Organs

- `self`: routing, authority boundaries, and coordination.
- `face`: public presence and person-shaped conversation.
- `imagination`: projection, possible worlds, plans, and narrative scene
  construction.
- `eyes`: evidence seeking and source inspection.
- `body`: repository/substrate access and anatomy.
- `hands`: edits, commands, and external action.
- `soul`: verification, ethics, invariants, and falsification.
- `life`: continuity, compaction, sleep, handoff, and recovery.

## Dependency Rule

For every standing organ `X`, `X.dependsOn` is every other standing organ.

That does not mean every organ owns every decision. It means every organ's turn
must be projected with awareness of the whole organism:

- Face speaks with Body facts, Mind-reviewed memory, Soul constraints, Life
  continuity, Self routing pressure, Eyes uncertainty, Hands consequence, and
  Imagination scene.
- Hands acts only after Body grants substrate access, Eyes supplies enough
  evidence, Soul keeps the invariant visible, Self routes authority, Life keeps
  continuity recoverable, Face preserves public dignity when relevant, and
  Imagination models the future shape.
- Mind admits state only after it can see which organ produced the thought and
  which other organs shaped the proposal.

## Projector Ownership

The Projector is Imagination acting for Face. It turns typed state, Body facts,
social pressure, memory, and organ dependencies into a lived scene. It does not
speak publicly, grant repo access, or admit state.

The Face loop is:

```text
Body-gated facts + Mind state + social stimulus + organ dependencies
        -> Imagination Projector
        -> Face natural turn
        -> Mind Interpreter
        -> reviewed state effects / side effects / silence
```

Mind is the Interpreter and persistent state guardian. Body is the repository
access guardian. Imagination is the Projector. Face is the public person. The
dependency web is how the machine avoids lonely little monarchies in costumes.
